About Our Data

Data Collection

Our datasets are collected through a comprehensive ecosystem monitoring network across Australia, using advanced remote sensing and field measurements.

Update Frequency

Datasets are updated quarterly with the most recent ecological indicators and changes. Historical data is archived and remains accessible.

Available Datasets

Woody Cover Fractions (WCF)

High-resolution woody vegetation cover fraction dataset derived from satellite imagery, representing tree canopy structure and density across Australia.

Updated: March 15, 2024 GeoTIFF, 1.8 GB Spatial Data
Download

Australian Forests

Comprehensive dataset of Australian forest ecosystems, including forest types, extent, condition, and multi-year deforestation monitoring (2020-2023).

Updated: February 28, 2024 Shapefile, 2.2 GB Forests

FAO Forests

Forest assessment data aligned with FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization) international standards, including climate impact metrics and carbon sequestration analysis.

Updated: January 20, 2024 ZIP, 1.5 GB International
