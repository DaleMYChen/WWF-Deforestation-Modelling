### Deforestation Modelling for WWF Australia
<img width="1414" height="695" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-21 at 2 55 42 pm" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/983addab-0089-45c8-a159-cee70e6744ac" />

[https://vmap.au/map]


**Objective**: Predict annual forests and therefore temporal trends of forest loss. Attribution and filtering of forest losses due to natural causes.  

**Method**: 
<br>
Random Forest Regression for Woody Coverage Prediction; 
<br>
Scientifically Informed Thresholding (Coverage -> Crown Covers -> Forests);
<br>
False Positives Removal with Ancillary Bushfire Datasets
